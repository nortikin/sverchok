Float to Integer¶

Functionality¶

Converts incoming Float values to the nearest whole number (Integer). Accepts lists and preserves levels of nestedness.

Inputs¶

A float, alone or in a list

Outputs¶

An int, alone or in a list

Examples¶

1.0 becomes 1
-1.9 becomes -2
4.3 becomes 4
4.7 becomes 5

[1.0, 3.0, 2.4, 5.7] becomes [1, 3, 2, 6]
